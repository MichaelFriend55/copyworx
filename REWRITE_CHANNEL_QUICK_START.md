## âœ¨ Best Practices

### Before You Rewrite

1. **Select the right text** - Choose complete thoughts, not fragments
2. **Pick the right channel** - Consider your audience and goal
3. **Review the result** - AI is smart but you know your brand best
4. **Edit if needed** - Feel free to tweak the AI output

### After Rewriting

1. **Check formatting** - Ensure line breaks work for the platform
2. **Verify hashtags** - Make sure they're relevant
3. **Test CTAs** - Ensure links and actions are clear
4. **A/B test** - Try different versions to see what works
